Team GFD

What is GFD?

GFD is a group that started on Gamemode 4 public season 3 with the founding Members: MightyBurger, InternetAlien, Sheepe and RedstoneSlimeMC.
However the story doesn't start at GFD as we know it, it instead start with the construction of EHXP, and a group looking into attaching farms called EHF (Extreme Hills Farming) set up by our founding members. They planned amongst other things to build a gold farm towering above EHXP, however EHF decided that it would be a bit of an eyesore and not enough space to cram all of this on top of EHXP. Red came up with another solution/suggestion build it all on top of his guardian farm. Liking this idea they moved over to his guardian farm, however no longer in extreme hills they decided they needed a new name, suggestions like GFS: Global Farming Society or GFS: Global Farming System flew out before Internet suggested the name we have grown to love GFD: Global Farming Department
From there GFD grew completing the laggest and cheapest place on the server. Then they decided to branch of GM4 and start on other projects creating Team GFD

Gamemode 4

Gamemode 4 is the origins of GFD where it started as the Global farming department, the one stop place for all your farming needs.

Game nights!

On our discord server we aim to host a public game night once a week around 7pm UTC. Although mainly base on mc the game nights do include over games!
